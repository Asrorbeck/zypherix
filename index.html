About Us

I'm Asrorbek Tursunpulatov, a passionate, self-proclaimed designer who specializes in full-stack development, particularly with React.js and Node.js. My enthusiasm lies in bridging the gap between technical and visual aspects of digital products, ensuring that every project I work on is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. I believe in the importance of user experience, pixel-perfect design, and writing clear, readable, and highly performant code.

My journey into the world of web development began in 2019, and since then, I've continually evolved as a developer, tackling new challenges and embracing the latest technologies. I studied Computer Science at INHA University in Tashkent from 2020 to 2024, where I built a strong foundation in the field. In 2021, I embarked on my frontend development journey at Najot Talim, a renowned IT training center, where I immersed myself in learning both frontend and backend technologies, with a particular focus on JavaScript, React.js, and Node.js. During this period, I gained practical experience as an intern at IT Park Tashkent, where I honed my frontend skills. Following that, I spent 6 months interning at the Ministry of Innovative Development, further refining my expertise.

After completing my studies, I worked as a freelancer for a year, applying my skills to various projects and building a diverse portfolio. My dedication and hard work led me to a role as a Frontend Developer at UTECHPRO Solutions, where I continue to contribute to cutting-edge web applications. Before joining UTECHPRO, I also interned as a Datacom Engineer at Huawei Uzbekistan for 4 months, where I expanded my knowledge of network technologies.
